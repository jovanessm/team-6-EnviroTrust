Climate Physics Engine
1
Build the industry baseline Simulate 30-year output from historical weather held flat — exactly what lenders assume today.
2
NOCT & Faiman temperature models NOCT — standard formula: sunlight + air temperature → panel heat.

Faiman — upgraded: adds real wind speed from satellite geometry for a more accurate cell temperature.
3
Apply climate warming year by year Two effects compound over 30 years:
Thermal derating — hotter panels produce less power.
Arrhenius degradation — hotter panels age faster.
4
Monte Carlo — 3,000 simulations Vary weather years, warming rates, and panel parameters to produce an honest probability range — not a single point estimate.
5
Translate to revenue Energy × SMARD electricity price (Germany mean 2018–2026, €93.4/MWh) = revenue at risk vs the industry number.
